# Positioning Plan

## Core Positioning

Use three generic lanes:

| Lane | Positioning | Avoided expectation |
|------|-------------|---------------------|
| Trading | Trading systems, broker APIs, dashboards, backtesting, execution tools | "AI will make profitable trades" |
| AI Agents | Workflow agents, research agents, CRM agents, ecommerce agents, internal ops copilots | "AI will generate trading returns" |
| Automation | API integrations, data pipelines, alerts, reports, business process automation | "magic AI outcome" |

The goal is to expand beyond trading without creating the impression that AI
will generate trading returns or that automation will solve vague business
problems without a defined workflow.

## Lane 1: Trading

Position trading as software engineering, not as AI alpha generation.

Good fit:

- broker API integrations
- exchange API integrations
- market-data pipelines
- execution dashboards
- backtesting systems
- strategy tooling
- TradingView, MetaTrader, NinjaTrader, Interactive Brokers, Alpaca, Binance
- scanners, alerts, portfolio tools, and risk tools

Buyer outcome:

- reliable trading infrastructure
- cleaner market-data workflows
- better testing and execution tooling
- dashboards and monitoring for trading activity
- automation around a strategy the client already owns

Avoided expectation:

> AI will make profitable trades.

Boundary language:

> I can build the trading software, data pipeline, execution workflow, or
> backtesting environment. I do not promise that an AI model or strategy will
> generate trading returns.

## Lane 2: AI Agents

Position AI agents as business workflow systems with clear inputs, outputs,
tools, and review points.

Good fit:

- workflow agents
- research agents
- reporting agents
- CRM agents
- sales ops copilots
- ecommerce agents
- support assistants
- internal ops copilots
- document and knowledge-base agents
- agents connected to APIs, databases, CRMs, email, Slack, Google Sheets,
  Airtable, Notion, or ecommerce platforms

Buyer outcome:

- reduce manual research and admin work
- summarize and structure information
- draft next actions
- update business systems
- monitor changes and create alerts
- produce outputs with human approval where needed

Avoided expectation:

> AI will generate trading returns.

Boundary language:

> I build AI agents for business workflows such as research, reporting, CRM,
> ecommerce, customer operations, and internal tools. I do not position AI
> agents as guaranteed trading-return systems.

## Lane 3: Automation

Position automation as deterministic workflow engineering: APIs, data movement,
alerts, reports, integrations, and repeatable business processes.

Good fit:

- API integrations
- data pipelines
- scheduled reports
- alerts and notifications
- webhook workflows
- Zapier, Make, n8n, Google Sheets, Airtable, Notion, Slack, email, and CRM
  integrations
- document parsing
- spreadsheet-to-system automation
- business process automation
- internal tooling around repeatable operations

Buyer outcome:

- remove repetitive manual steps
- move data between systems reliably
- create reports and alerts automatically
- reduce operational errors
- make existing tools work together

Avoided expectation:

> Magic AI outcome.

Boundary language:

> I can automate a defined workflow with clear triggers, data sources, actions,
> and success criteria. If the process is unclear, the first step is mapping the
> workflow before building the automation.

## Jobs To Avoid

- AI trading bot promising profit
- "guaranteed returns" trading systems
- vague AI alpha-generation projects
- generic chatbot projects with no clear workflow or data source
- automation jobs where the buyer cannot define the workflow
- low-budget website or Shopify setup work
- pure visual design work with no automation or engineering component

## Operating Rule

Keep the lanes separate in client communication:

- Trading means software and infrastructure.
- AI Agents means business workflow intelligence.
- Automation means deterministic process execution.

Do not let clients blend these into a promise that AI will produce trading
returns or that automation will magically fix an undefined process.
